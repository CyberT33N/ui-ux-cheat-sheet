## Overview

Add to Cart buttons are essential UI components for e-commerce websites that initiate the process of adding products to a shopping cart. These buttons often include animations, icons, or micro-interactions to provide visual feedback when clicked. Effective Add to Cart buttons stand out visually from surrounding content, clearly communicate their purpose, and provide immediate feedback to confirm successful interactions.
